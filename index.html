CalSTRS · Internal Communications

Fraud Squad: Law & Order Edition

Senior leadership from the Service Retirement division wanted a creative way to introduce the division's fraud awareness unit and increase staff engagement with an otherwise routine topic. I developed a Law & Order style parody from concept to final production, including scriptwriting, filming, directing, and editing. The video was presented to more than 80 employees at an all-staff meeting and received recognition from the Service Retirement Director for its creativity and effectiveness.
